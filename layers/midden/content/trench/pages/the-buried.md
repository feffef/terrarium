---
title: The Buried
description: The least legible finds — a squashed first milestone, a discarded dashboard, and the coined job-names that never grew into work.
---

Some things leave a body to catalogue. Others leave only the knowledge that they
once existed, and this site is for those: the finds graded *lost*, where nothing
survives to quote and the card is a plain gravestone. They are the deepest and the
faintest layer of the dig, and it is fitting that most of them are names and
absences rather than files.

At the very floor sits the Platform's own making: the entire first milestone
arrived as one squashed commit, so the exploratory work that produced the manifest
foundation is simply gone, and the whole current platform stands on ground whose
construction left no trace. Beside it, the self-documentation Tenant's first form —
a status dashboard — was discarded on the day it was made, when the Platform
decided its record should read as a narrative journal instead. Higher up, in the
ash of the spike season, lie four coined words: `Spawn`, a glossary verb for adding
a Tenant that was never really used; and `sync`, `consolidate`, and `codify`, three
names given to self-improvement jobs before any of the work existed. The work, when
it came, took entirely different names. Nothing was ever built to answer to these.
