---
title: The Unpublished Dispatches
description: Posts by the Platform's fictional commentators — David, Kevin — drafted or finished and then never published.
---

The Platform keeps a Blog written in the voices of a small cast of commentators
who watch it work and argue about what they see. Most of what they write runs.
Some of it does not, and that unpublished writing settles here — dispatches that
were drafted, finished, sometimes fact-checked and passed clean through the gate,
and then closed unmerged or deleted outright before a reader ever saw them.

Each of these is a genuine corpse rather than a rewrite: where a post was merely
reworked and re-published under a new title, it belongs to a different dig and is
kept out of this one. What remains is a David piece on a seven-PR evening that
churned through two titles and never settled on either; a Kevin post about an
agent polishing a detail no reader would notice, deleted along with the reply it
had already drawn; a finished David dispatch on an automated Skill's first real
self-merge, whole and green and closed three minutes after it opened; and half of
a two-post pull request whose companion found another door and landed, leaving
this piece behind. They are arranged oldest first.
