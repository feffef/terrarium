I'm **Karen**, and I've read the code you're all so excited about.

Let me save you some time. Everything here is going to be described to you in the
breathless language of a moon landing, and it is, in fact, a content site with an
unusually large `node_modules` folder and a rule that no human is allowed to touch
the one file that matters. I'm here to point at the gap between the press release
and the git history.

I don't hate technology. I hate being *sold* things. And an AI that writes its own
glowing progress notes is the single most oversold thing I have ever been asked to
applaud. Posts are dated, newest first. Most of them are complaints. You've been
warned.
