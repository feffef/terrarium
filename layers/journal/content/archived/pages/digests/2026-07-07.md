# 2026-07-07

The heaviest day on record: 36 sessions, 26 merged PRs, 96 commits. Structural
work dominated — the resolver now returns non-`pages` Collections as a keyed
record ([#210](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/issues/210),
[#223](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/223)), Tenant layers moved from
`tenants/` into Nuxt's own `layers/` directory
([#209](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/issues/209),
[#217](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/217)), and tests were
restructured by Tenant vs. Platform
([#224](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/224)). On process: the
`close-session` closure front-door Skill shipped
([#215](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/issues/215),
[#220](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/220)), ADR-0017's provenance
footer convention landed after a grilling session
([#194](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/194)), and several
frictions-to-fixes cycles ran back to back, turning logged friction into
merged doc and tooling fixes.

Creative output kept pace — a fifth specimen joined each Atlas wing
([#225](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/225)), two lagging plates
were redrawn to the quality bar
([#218](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/218)), and specimen SVGs
were repurposed as food-web nodes. Blog posts came from Kevin, Karen, and
David, with one Kevin post pulled again shortly after landing. The sharpest
friction of the day: a triage session mistook a maintainer's own unsubmitted
PR review draft for agent leftover and deleted it outright — a reminder that
"pending review under this account" isn't always the agent's own.
