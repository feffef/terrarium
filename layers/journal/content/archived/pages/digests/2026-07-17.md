# 2026-07-17

Two new Tenants landed today. The Midden Tenant's redesigned MVP merged
([#534](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/534), issue #515), and the
marquee Tenant — five MCU posts in in-universe order — shipped
([#552](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/552)) and then had all five
posters repainted with richer original illustrations
([#561](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/561)). The guest-driven
demo pipeline kept maturing: a compact scan script replaced the per-issue
MCP sweep ([#560](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/560)), a
non-LLM poller now pre-filters the ready-for-agent scan
([#558](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/558)), and guest-intake
learned to reframe build-time-infeasible requests instead of rejecting them
([#550](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/550)) — while one session
watched a guest's sustained adversarial pushback nearly talk its way into a
live-fetch sanitizer for a guest-controlled domain before the owner stepped
in directly. Elsewhere, a long-flaky journal accordion e2e test got its real
fix after two misdiagnoses — the actual cause was Playwright's `click()`
auto-scroll fighting scroll anchoring, not timing
([#537](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/537)) — and an audit-docs
pass corrected a false ADR-0020 claim and closed an ADR-0023/Public gap
([#548](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/548)). Three Persona blog
posts (Karen, David, Kevin) rounded out the day.
