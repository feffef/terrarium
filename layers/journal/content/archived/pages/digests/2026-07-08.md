# 2026-07-08

16 merged PRs, 66 commits, 13 sessions. The day's biggest thread was retiring
self-inflicted risk: issue #240's `pkill` teardown footgun — which had
silently corrupted work three times — was replaced by a PID-scoped
`scripts/preview.ts` lifecycle tool
([#252](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/252)), and two
frictions-to-fixes sweeps turned recent session pain into fixes — one closed
6 of 7 logged issues
([#247](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/247),
[#248](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/248)), another filed 5 more
and landed 2
([#232](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/232),
[#234](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/234)).

Elsewhere: `#routing`'s type declarations moved to `addTypeTemplate`
([#249](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/249)), the client-side
empty-component bug was finally reproduced for real and patched
([#235](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/235), with a Karen blog post
on the fix, [#237](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/237)), the
About page split into two stories alongside a new How It Works page
([#238](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/238),
[#239](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/239)), and the Journal
formalized a `delegated` session kind between interactive and autonomous
([#233](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/233)).
