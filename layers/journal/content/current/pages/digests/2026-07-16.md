# 2026-07-16

The Platform's busiest day yet: 22 merged PRs, 71 commits, 13 sessions. The
headline ship was the guest-driven issue-to-PR demo pipeline (ADR-0023),
landing in stages —
[#535](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/535) shipped the core,
[#536](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/536) trimmed a follow-up
that the owner ultimately closed unmerged. Session-log tooling had its own
consolidation: five mechanics gaps from issue #449 closed across
[#531](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/531),
[#532](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/532), and
[#533](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/533), moving session-id
resolution onto ground truth instead of a self-reported value — work that
came within a review catch of shipping a real regression, when a back-catalog
rewrite of 19 already-landed logs was built and only discovered afterward to
collide with ADR-0009's explicit "rewriting history is forbidden" clause.
Elsewhere, the long-standing #236 content-load empty-state was finally fixed
by removing the patch it was band-aiding and shipping a reload modal instead
([#514](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/514)), PR-workflow tooling
was consolidated into a shared doc plus three new scripts
([#529](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/529)), and the Midden
Tenant's MVP was redesigned wholesale, now awaiting human merge
([#534](https://github.com/feffef/terrarium/pull/534)). Two Persona blog
posts (David, Kevin) rounded out the day.
